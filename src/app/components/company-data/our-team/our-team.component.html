Our Team

Chandrashekar Katipelli

Co-Founder & CEO

Chandrashekar Katipelli is the Co-founder and the CEO of Metdine. Besides his role at Metdine, Chandrashekar is also the Managing Director of IndianFolk.com, a platform for opinion, analysis and commentary. Chandrashekar has also written a book, 'Gandhi & Lincoln', on the occasion of Mahatma Gandhi's 150th birth anniversary. Prior to moving to India, Chandrashekar worked for the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and VeriSign in the United States. Chandrashekar has a bachelors degree from Osmania University College of Engineering and an MBA in Finance from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, United States.

Rekha Marri

Co-founder & CPO

Rekha Marri is a Co-founder and Chief Product Officer of Metdine. Prior to Metdine, Rekha worked as a Software Engineer and Quality Assurance Director for Fourth Wall Media in the United States for many years before moving to India. Rekha has a bachelor's degree in computer science engineering from Gulbarga University and a masters degree in the same field from Texas A&M Kingsville in the United States.
